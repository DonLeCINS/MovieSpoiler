Plot

In this movie a child growing up faces many different problems during his life, but doesn't let much get in his way. While growing up forrest falls in love with a girl named Jenny, but she always runs away and tells Forrest to do his own things. Although unlucky himself he stays motivated, shows his love and is always caring for others. In the end, Forrest ends up marrying Jenny and have a child together. Source:
